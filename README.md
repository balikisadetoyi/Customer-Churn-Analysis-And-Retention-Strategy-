# Telriva Telecom - Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy
## Overview
Telriva Telecom is losing customers. This end to end project analyses who is churning, why they are leaving, and what retention strategies can reduce churn. Built using SQL Server for data cleaning and analysis and Power BI for visualisation.

**Overall churn rate: 26.5% - 1 in 4 customers have left the business.**
## Tools
SQL Server · Power BI · DAX · Power Query

## Data Source
IBM Telco Customer Churn dataset (expanded community version) sourced from Kaggle. The dataset contains 7,043 customers across 50 columns covering demographics, services, billing and churn status.  
## Business Questions
This analysis was structured around 12 business questions covering overall churn rate, customer demographics, contract type, tenure, internet type, monthly charges, service usage, promotional offers, satisfaction scores, referral programme engagement, extra data charges and geographic distribution.
## Key Findings
- New customers churn at 47.4% - the first year is the highest risk period
- Month to month contracts drive 45.8% churn vs 2.5% for two year contracts
- Senior citizens churn at 41.7% - significantly above the 26.5% average
- Fiber optic customers have the highest churn rate at 40.7%
- Offer E underperforms at 52.9% churn while Offer A retains at 6.7%
- Online Security subscribers churn at just 14.6%
- Referral programme customers churn at 19.3% vs 32.5% for non enrolled
- San Diego churn rate of 64.9% - highest of all major cities
## Recommendations
Findings from this analysis led to ten retention strategy recommendations covering early customer onboarding, loyalty programmes for high value customers, incentives for long term contracts, targeted support for senior citizens, fiber optic service quality improvements, promotional offer redesign, referral programme expansion, protective service bundling, customer satisfaction improvements and regional intervention in San Diego.
## Dashboard
Three page interactive Power BI dashboard covering Customer Overview, Churn Drivers and Services and Engagement. A benchmark reference line at 26.5% is applied across all churn rate charts to contextualise findings against the company average. Dashboard PDF available in repository.

## Limitations
Data covers a single quarter,Q3 and may not reflect seasonal trends. Geographic analysis is limited to cities with 30 or more customers to ensure statistical reliability. All monetary values are in USD.
